About Me

My name is Marian O. Rahman, I am enthusiastic professional individual who is seeking a new challenging career after many years of being a Computer Systems Administrator, Technical Writer and a Document Specialist. I am a hardworking individual who strives to achieve the best in all areas, and I am always eager to learn new skills and advance my career. As I look at this opportunity as huge career change I have completed a training in Web Development, and I am eager to build upon my knowledge and continue to learn within this sector. As a self-motivated individual, I am keen to follow my dream within this new industry of Web Development and software Engineering. As the system administrator in the Quality Control Department I have had several positions and responsibilities in my career such as document Specialist, in this position I created approved and distributed all technical specification through the system and built workflow approval systems for both internal departments and outside Vendors. , I was responsible for creating user manuals, trained users in the system, trouble shoot the system and also assist users with questions on the system. I created deviations and waivers and controlled these documentations electronically. I was a part of the SAP PLM implementation team, my responsibilities was to interview users and find out their pain point and find out how we can make the systems better for them. I was part of the user acceptance and unit testing team. As part of the team I wrote test scripts and participated in testing. I was responsible for creating the user manuals and training users in the new system.
